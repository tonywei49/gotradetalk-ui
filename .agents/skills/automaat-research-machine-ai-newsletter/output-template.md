# Newsletter Output Template

Use this template for generating the weekly AI newsletter.

---

```markdown
# 📬 AI Weekly Newsletter — Week {WNN}, {YYYY}

**Period:** {Mon DD} - {Sun DD Mon YYYY}
**Stories curated:** {N} from {M} daily digests

---

## 🎯 This Week in AI

{2-3 sentence editorial opening. Capture the week's narrative arc. Reference the biggest story. Set the tone.}

---

## {emoji} {Theme Title 1}

{1-2 sentence context for this theme}

- [ ] **{Story Title}** — {enhanced summary, expand beyond original 1-liner} [[Source]({URL})] | *{Mon DD}*
- [ ] **{Story Title}** — {enhanced summary} [[Source]({URL})] | *{Mon DD}*

**Why this matters:** {2-3 sentence analysis connecting stories, implications for engineers/industry}

---

## {emoji} {Theme Title 2}

{1-2 sentence context}

- [ ] **{Story Title}** — {enhanced summary} [[Source]({URL})] | *{Mon DD}*
- [ ] **{Story Title}** — {enhanced summary} [[Source]({URL})] | *{Mon DD}*

**Why this matters:** {analysis}

---

## ⚡ Quick Hits

- **{Story}** — {1-line summary} [[Source]({URL})]

---
```

---

## Section Guidelines

### This Week in AI (Editorial)

- Sets the narrative tone for the newsletter
- Reference the single most impactful story
- 2-3 sentences max, opinionated voice OK
- Capture the "feel" of the week

### Themed Sections

- 2-4 themes depending on story count
- Narrative titles, NOT digest section copies
- Each theme: context → stories → analysis
- Enhanced summaries: expand 1-liners into 2-3 sentences
- Include date for each story (`| *Mon DD*`)
- "Why this matters" connects the dots

### Quick Hits

- Overflow stories that don't fit themes
- 1-line per story, no analysis
- Still include source URLs
- Skip if all stories fit into themes

### Looking Ahead

- Forward-looking, not backward summary
- Based on patterns from this week's curated stories
- 2-3 bullets max

---

## Formatting Rules

1. **Emojis** — Theme headers get contextual emojis
2. **Bullets** — Prefer over paragraphs
3. **Links** — Every story needs source URL
4. **Dates** — Each story tagged with its digest date
5. **Tags** — `#ai-newsletter #weekly #YYYY-WNN`
6. **Wikilinks** — Connect to vault notes where relevant

---

## Scaling Guidelines

| Story Count | Themes | Quick Hits | Looking Ahead |
|-------------|--------|------------|---------------|
| 1-2         | None (simple list) | None | 1-2 bullets |
| 3-7         | 2 | Optional | 2-3 bullets |
| 8-15        | 2-4 | Yes | 2-3 bullets |
| 16+         | 3-4 | Yes (generous) | 3 bullets |
